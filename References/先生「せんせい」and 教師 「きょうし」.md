Reference:
[Understanding the difference between '先生' and '教師' in Japanese](https://nihongo-net.com/2024/11/03/understanding-the-difference-between-sensei-and-kyoshi/)

1. Both terms can describe a teaching position
2. You address your teacher as「先生」directly
3. You can use「先生」to respectfully address other specialists, including doctors, politicians, and writers

However「教師」is **only** used to designate school teachers.
